The Future Foundation, commonly reffered to as FF for short, is a Super Smash Bros Facebook Clan founded by Alejandro Jimenez. There's a strong belief within the team that anyone can become better at the game regardless of their current skill level. This makes the team open for anyone to join the already growing clan.

Facebook

The Future Foundation was born in a Facebook page with a competitive focus on Crew Battles. Every team needed some sort of mascot so FF chose Danganronpa. Individual wars and teams can be found on the FB page for the Smash Bros Clan Wars.
